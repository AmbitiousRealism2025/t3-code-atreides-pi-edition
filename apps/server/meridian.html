Features

Everything your remote team needs

Stop losing hours to status meetings. Meridian gives you the insights of a daily standup in a fraction of the time.

🤖

AI-Powered Summaries

Our AI reads every team update and distills them into a crisp, actionable digest — so managers stay informed without reading dozens of messages.

  • Smart blockers detection
  • Sentiment & morale tracking
  • Auto-generated weekly reports
  • Digest delivered to your inbox
🌍

Timezone-Aware Scheduling

Meridian automatically sends prompts at the right local time for each team member — no more 2 AM check-ins or missed reminders.

  • Per-user custom submission windows
  • Smart deadline detection
  • Holiday & OOO awareness
  • Overlap visualizer for leads

Slack & Teams Integration

Standups happen right inside the tools your team already uses. No new tab, no new app — just a simple prompt where work already happens.

  • One-click Slack & MS Teams install
  • Thread-based responses
  • Channel summary posts
  • Jira & Linear auto-linking
